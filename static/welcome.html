MetaboFlow aims at building, testing and delivering cloud-based Galaxy workflow(s), with the computational capacity to process datasets with 1000's of samples and simultaneously capture all metadata associated to allow rigorous reproducibility and reporting.

MetaboFlow infrastructure is formulated based on several community agreed processing, feature extraction and compound identification tools. The necessary functionality is incorporated so that it can be readily used in conjunction with popular online metabolomics databases such as MetaboLights.

Metabolights-labs.org is maintained by the Metabolights team at the EMBL-EBI . If you have an issue, please get in touch at metabolights-help@ebi.ac.uk
